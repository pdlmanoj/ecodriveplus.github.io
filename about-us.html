About Us

Welcome to EcoDrive Plus, the premier vehicle fleet tracking system designed to revolutionize the way you manage your fleet. We are a dedicated team of passionate individuals who have come together to develop a cutting-edge solution that empowers businesses to enhance their operational efficiency, maximize productivity, and prioritize sustainability. At EcoDrive Plus, we understand the challenges faced by fleet managers and the critical need for real-time information to make informed decisions. Our comprehensive platform offers a range of features that cater to your fleet management needs, providing you with the tools to optimize your operations and ensure a seamless experience.

Vehicle Details: Our platform provides detailed insights into each vehicle in your fleet. From important information such as vehicle make, model, and year to more specific details like maintenance history and fuel consumption, you can effortlessly track and manage your vehicles' performance. Stay informed about the status of each vehicle and take proactive measures to keep your fleet running smoothly.

Overview: With our intuitive dashboard, you can get a holistic overview of your entire fleet at a glance. Monitor key metrics, such as vehicle utilization, mileage, and driver behavior, in real-time. The customizable interface allows you to prioritize the data that matters most to you, enabling you to make data-driven decisions and drive your business forward.

Reports: Our robust reporting feature provides in-depth analytics and generates comprehensive reports for your fleet. Analyze trends, identify areas for improvement, and evaluate the performance of your vehicles and drivers. Gain valuable insights into fuel efficiency, maintenance costs, and driver behavior to optimize your fleet management strategies.

Tracking:: Track the location of your vehicles in real-time with our advanced GPS tracking technology. Monitor routes, detect deviations, and ensure efficient navigation. The live tracking feature allows you to respond promptly to any unforeseen circumstances, reducing response times and improving customer satisfaction.


At EcoDrive Plus, we are committed to providing you with an exceptional user experience and delivering unparalleled customer support. Our dedicated team is always available to assist you with any questions or concerns you may have. We strive to continually enhance our platform with the latest technological advancements to meet the evolving needs of your business.
